Physical Fitness

Rehabilitation exercises are structured physical activities designed to help individuals
recover from injuries, surgeries, or medical conditions. These exercises aim to restore
strength, mobility, flexibility, and function while minimizing pain and preventing future
injuries. They are typically tailored to the individual's needs and may involve a
combination of physical therapy, stretching, strengthening, balance training, and endurance
exercises. Rehabilitation exercises are crucial for improving the quality of life, promoting
independence, and ensuring a safe and effective recovery process.
